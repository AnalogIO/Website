Analog IO

What Is IO?

Analog IO is a "coding club" at the IT University of Copenhagen where students voluntarily meet and code on projects that benefit the student environment.
The students get free coffee from Café Analog during meet-ups and get to improve their coding and organizational skills in a casual environment.
